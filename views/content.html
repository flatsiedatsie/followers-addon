Missing permission to read and control devices

To use followers you will have to give it permission to access your devices. Followers currently does not have permission.

The Candle app store can give Followers this permission. Visit the settings page for this addon via the Candle app store, and allow this addon to access your things. Then click save.

If you don't have the Candle App Store, go to Settings -> Developer and then Create Local Authorization. Generate a key there by clicking on Allow at the bottom of the page. Copy the token that's generated, and then visit the settings page for this addon. There you can paste in the code, and click Apply to save it.

Followers allows you to have the value of one property 'follow' the value of another.

For example, you could have your livingroom light's brightness be applied to the hallway light too, so their brightness always changes in step.

You could even reverse this relation, so that as the brightness of one light goes down, the brightness of the other goes up.

Debugging is enabled

This addon is currently set to save detailed information about its operation in the internal logs. This may impact your privacy, as this also means that insights into your behaviour might be stored in those logs.

You can disable debugging mode by going to Settings, then Addons, and then clicking on the Settings button for the Followers addon. There you can uncheck the debugging option and click Apply.
